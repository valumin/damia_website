Overview
TANKSPY

Our product TANKSPY is a wireless high-tech liquid level-measuring device that can provide real-time measurement of the level and quantity in water and diesel tanks. The device helps end users to monitor water and diesel supply and storage, and manage consumption especially with the ‘Low Quantity’ feature that can be set at the desired level and provides early alert when the quantity reaches a certain level.

IT'S JUST EASIER.

With billions of domestic water and diesel tanks around the world, TANKSPY is the solution to monitor liquid quantities without the need for frequent trips to the roof, or the basement.
